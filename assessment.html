Lake Resilience Assessment

1. How close is the lake to your home or area that you visit frequently?

2. How clear is the water in the lake from what you observe?

3. How often do you see people involved in activities near the lake?

4. How often does the lake experience flooding or drought conditions?

5. How diverse is the wildlife in the lake and surrounding areas?

6. How much pollution is present in the lake (e.g., trash, chemicals)?

7. Is the lake used for recreational activities (swimming, boating)?

8. Is there any noticeable pollution from nearby industries?
