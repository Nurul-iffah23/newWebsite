What is TEK?
TEK (Traditional Ecological Knowledge) is the wisdom that local communities have about their environment, passed down through generations. It helps people manage natural resources like water, land, and wildlife in a sustainable way.

Why should people know about TEK?
Knowing about TEK is important because it offers practical and proven methods for protecting nature. TEK helps preserve biodiversity, protect ecosystems, and make communities stronger against challenges like climate change and resource loss. It combines old knowledge with modern science to create better solutions for the future.
