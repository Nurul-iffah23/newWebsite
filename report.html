Why Should You Care About Lake Resilience?

Lake resilience is essential for maintaining biodiversity, supporting local communities, and ensuring sustainable ecosystems. Healthy lakes contribute to clean water, recreation, and climate stability. Understanding and improving lake resilience helps protect these vital natural resources for future generations.

Who to Contact?

If you notice changes or issues in your local lake, such as pollution, declining water levels, or harmful algal blooms, please report them to the relevant authorities.
